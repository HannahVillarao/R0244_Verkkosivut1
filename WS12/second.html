About Earth

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only celestial body known to harbor life. Its unique characteristics make it a haven for biodiversity, supporting over 8 billion humans and countless other species. The Modern English word Earth developed, via Middle English, from an Old English noun most often spelled eorðe.

Unique Features of Earth

Vast Oceans

Earth is covered by over 70% water, providing a habitat for marine life and regulating global climate. The ocean influences and regulates Earth's climate, acting like the planet's 'heart.'

Life-supporting Atmosphere

Earth's atmosphere contains the perfect mix of gases necessary for life. These gases are found in atmospheric layers (troposphere, stratosphere, mesosphere, thermosphere, and exosphere) defined by unique features such as temperature and pressure.
